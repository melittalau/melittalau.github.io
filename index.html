About me

I am a German Designer based in Berlin.
My design career started as a web designer in 2013. Since then, I have found myself exploring the various disciplines of design, working as an in-house designer in different medium-sized e-commerce companies and start-ups, with diverse products, challenges and customer needs. In 2019 I officially transitioned into the field of user experience design. My expertise is in the UX of product discovery and findability and design systems.
To design digital solutions that are enjoyable and easy to use, I rely on a balance of data-driven decisions and empathy for user needs. In my process, I invite different teams to participate in the creative development and excite my colleagues for usability and the user's perspective. Due to my knowledge and curiosity in web dev and frontend trends, I foster a close relationship with development teams and ensure each project’s technical performance, accessibility and feasibility.
My expertise includes skills such as user research, UX design methods, prototyping and user testing, responsive UI design, A/B tests with UX metrics, design systems and many more.
